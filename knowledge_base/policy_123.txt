**Standard Auto Insurance Policy: #POL-12345**

**Section 1: Covered Vehicles**
- 2022 White SUV (VIN: ...123)
- 2021 Blue Sedan (VIN: ...456)

**Section 2: Liability Coverage (At-Fault)**
- 2.1: We cover bodily injury and property damage to OTHERS when the insured driver is legally liable (at fault).
- 2.2: Covered accident types include: Rear-end collisions, T-Bone (Side-Impact), Sideswipes, and Intersection accidents is covered.
- 2.3: Exclusions: Incidents resulting from illegal activities (e.g., street racing) or intentional acts are NOT covered.

**Section 3: Collision Coverage (Damage to Your Car)**
- 3.1: We pay for damage to the insured vehicle caused by a collision with another vehicle or object, regardless of fault.
- 3.2: If another driver is at fault (e.g., they T-Bone you), this policy covers your repairs initially, subject to a $500 deductible, while we pursue the other driver's insurance (Subrogation).

**Section 4: Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist**
- 4.1: If the insured vehicle is hit by a driver who is at fault but has no insurance (or unknown, e.g., Hit and Run), this policy provides coverage for bodily injury and vehicle repairs.
